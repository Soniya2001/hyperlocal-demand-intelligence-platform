Project Summary

OneMindAI is a hyperlocal demand intelligence platform designed to help retailers identify supply-demand imbalances across regions and optimize inventory distribution using artificial intelligence.

Retail businesses often struggle with uneven product demand across locations. Some regions experience stock shortages while others hold excess inventory, resulting in lost revenue, increased holding costs, and inefficient supply chains. OneMindAI addresses this problem by analyzing historical sales and inventory data to detect demand patterns at a regional level.

The platform applies machine learning models to forecast future demand for each product and region, enabling businesses to anticipate demand fluctuations. It then identifies slow-moving inventory and surplus stock locations. Using a hyperlocal matching engine, the system recommends intelligent redistribution of inventory from surplus regions to high-demand areas.

To enhance decision-making, the platform integrates Generative AI through AWS Bedrock to generate executive-level strategic insights. These insights summarize operational risks, estimate potential financial impact, and recommend optimal redistribution strategies.

The prototype demonstrates how AI-driven supply chain intelligence can reduce lost revenue, lower inventory holding costs, and improve overall retail efficiency. By enabling proactive, data-driven decisions, OneMindAI empowers retailers to optimize inventory flow across geographically distributed markets.
